Knit Wit Page

Knitting is a method by which yarn is manipulated to create a textile or fabric. It is used in many types of garments.

Knitting may be done by hand or by machine. Knitting creates stitches: loops of yarn in a row, either flat or in the round.

There are usually many active stitches on the knitting needle at one time
